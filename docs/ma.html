Managing to meet

Meeting a stranger

You are meeting a stranger in Chicago.

You both know that you need to meet each other today, but you haven’t agreed on a place or a time and you have no means of communication. Remember, you cannot communicate with them now or previously in any way!

Where do you go to meet a stranger?

When do you meet them?

Response Breakdown

Times

32.84% (22 out of 67) of people chose to meet at exactly 12:00.

52.24% chose another time “on the hour” (but not 12:00).

13.43% chose a time “on the half hour.”

Just 1.49% (1 lonely person) chose anything else.

Locations (Grouped)

The most common response for where to meet a stranger in Chicago was The Bean, with 22 responses (34.92%).

Common responses of Chicago landmarks included the Bean/Cloudgate (\(N\) = 22), Millennium/Grant Park (\(N\) = 9), and the Sears/Willis Tower (\(N\) = 1).

Meeting an unfamiliar student

You are meeting another UChicago student who you have not met before.

You both know that you are both UChicago students and that you should meet today, but you haven’t agreed on a meeting location and you have no means of communication.

Where do you go to meet an unknown UChicago student?

Response Breakdown

Locations (Grouped)

The most common response for where to meet an unknown UChicago student in Chicago was The Reg, with 21 responses (31.82%). By comparison, only 1 respondents (1.59%) chose to meet a stranger at The Reg.

While The Bean was the most common response for where to meet a stranger in Chicago, no respondents chose to meet an unfamiliar UChicago student at The Bean.

Meeting your UChicago friend

You are meeting your closest friend at UChicago.

You haven’t agreed on a meeting location and you have no means of communication.

Where do you go to meet your closest UChicago friend?

Response Breakdown

Locations (Grouped)

The most common response for where to meet a close UChicago friend in Chicago was Other, with 14 responses (20.9%). By comparison, only 9 respondents (14.29%) chose to meet a stranger at Other and only 5 respondents (7.58%) chose to meet an unknown student at Other.

While The Bean was the most common response for where to meet a stranger in Chicago, no respondents chose to meet a close UChicago friend at The Bean.

The Reg was the most common response for where to meet an unfamiliar UChicago student in Chicago, but only 6 respondents (8.96%) chose to meet a close UChicago friend at The Reg.

Coin Flip

You and an anonymous partner need to choose heads or tails.

Same

Aim to match.

If you both choose the SAME answer, you both win. Which do you choose?

Different

Aim to differ.

If you both choose the DIFFERENT answers, you both win. Which do you choose?

Numbers

0 to 10

Pick a number 0 to 10.

You win if you match your partner.

Fixed set

Select a number from the set.

You win if you match your partner.

Bigger the better

Pick any number.

You win if you match your partner, and the bigger the number, the more you both win.
